Light and shading


Light and shading are the key of the party. Sunny day? Nighttime? Maybe just trying to emulate a LED strip?

By enabling the Plain Shading checkbox on the menu, you'll see that the character now has flat shading. If you disable it, it now has shadowed shading. That's all for the shading part!

Talking about the lighting part, there's a textbox below that checkbox, which specifies an hexadecimal in 0xRRGGBB format.
0x indicates Hexadecimal, RR is Red, GG Green and BB Blue. Modify as you wish.

By applying now you should see the light has changed. Ta da! That's all for the light aswell!

One last thing, the defaults are Shadowed Shading and white light. Enjoy your new tonality... O_o
